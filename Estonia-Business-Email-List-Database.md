### **Legal compliance**

All marketing emails must comply with laws like CAN-SPAM, CASL, and GDPR. *Definition:* For example, the U.S. **CAN-SPAM Act** requires accurate header information, a valid physical address, and a clear unsubscribe link in each commercial email. Canada’s **CASL** similarly mandates consent (express or implied) and an unsubscribe option in every message. In the EU, **GDPR** demands explicit opt-in for marketing emails and easy withdrawal of consent. Since Estonia is in the EU, GDPR and the ePrivacy rules apply: lists must originate from compliant sources and include built-in opt-outs to avoid legal issues. Providers like LeadsBlue explicitly state that their data is **GDPR-compliant**, and they often document consent collection. When using a list, always honor unsubscribe requests promptly and keep records of consent where needed.
